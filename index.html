Best Facility Management Company Across Gurgaon, Noida & Delhi NCR

Tuskers was founded by Major R.K. Rao (Veteran) in 2008 after serving the Indian Army for more than 29 years. It is the origins as a coinage of an Ex-Army Officer that Tuskers has established a reputation for providing quality services to numerous institutional clients.
We at Tuskers India aim to provide all your Security and Facility management needs under one roof.
